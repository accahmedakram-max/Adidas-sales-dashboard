# Adidas US Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This interactive Excel dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of **Adidas sales performance** across the United States for the period of 2020–2021. It tracks key performance indicators (KPIs) such as total revenue, operating profit, and regional retailer performance, offering actionable insights into product trends and sales channel efficiency.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Total Sales:** $120,166,650
* **Total Profit:** $332,134,761.45
* **Top Retailer:** West Gear (26% of total sales)
* **Leading Product Category:** Men's Street Footwear ($82.8M in profit)

---

## Dashboard Features & Visualizations

### 1. Sales Distribution by Channel
A **Doughnut Chart** breaks down how customers are purchasing Adidas products:
* **Online:** 37% (Leading channel)
* **Outlet:** 33%
* **In-store:** 30%

### 2. Retailer Performance Analysis
* **Market Share:** A Pie Chart illustrates sales distribution among major retailers, with **West Gear** (26%) and **Foot Locker** (24%) dominating the market.
* **Stacked Column Chart:** Shows the breakdown of sales methods (In-store, Online, Outlet) for each specific retailer, highlighting that West Gear has the highest volume of In-store sales.

### 3. Product Profitability
A **Bar Chart** ranks the profitability of different product lines. 
* **Top Performer:** Men's Street Footwear.
* **Lowest Performer:** Women's Athletic Footwear.

### 4. Time-Series Trends (2020 - 2021)
A **Line Graph** tracks the *Sum of Total Sales* vs. *Sum of Operating Profit* over eight quarters. 
* **Insight:** There is a significant growth spike starting in **Q1 2021**, with profit margins expanding rapidly compared to the previous year.

### 5. Interactive Slicers
The dashboard includes dynamic filters (Slicers) to allow users to drill down into specific data points:
* **Retailer Slicer:** Amazon, Foot Locker, Kohl's, Sports Direct, Walmart, West Gear.
* **Product Slicer:** Filters by category (e.g., Men's vs. Women's Apparel/Footwear).

---

## Technical Stack
* **Tool:** Microsoft Excel
* **Features Used:** Pivot Tables, Power Query (for data cleaning), Slicers, Advanced Charting, and Dashboard UI Design.
